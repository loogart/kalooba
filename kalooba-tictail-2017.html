What is Kalooba?

Kalooba sells exclusive designs by Loogart on the highest quality apparel. The designs are inspired by the cityscapes of Canadian cities

Bold design.
Fun illustrations.

Canadian artist Loogart re-invented the cityscape of Montreal into playful, geometric and minimal illustrations that provide a fresh visual he named CityLines. With these fresh designs, Kalooba has partnered up with Loogart to produce a selection of their cityscape-themed illustrations on high-quality apparel and products.

Where can you buy Kalooba stuff?

You can find Kalooba online at Kalooba.ca. Moreover, Kalooba products can be purchased at the following points of sale (listed below).

  • Montréal-Pierre Elliott Trudeau International Airport
  • Ottawa MacDonald-Cartier International Airport
  • Toronto Pearson International Airport
  • Art Pop Montréal (129 Avenue du Mont-Royal E, Montréal, QC H2T 1N9)
  • Blue Banana Market (250 Augusta Avenue, Toronto, ON)
  • Spacing Store (401 Richmond St W, Toronto, ON)

For wholesale opportunities or partnerships, please visit our {{#lang}}B2B section{{/lang}}.

Want to carry Kalooba in your store?

Interested in selling Kalooba products in your store or business? If you are a Canadian clothing store, a tourism shop or a trendy boutique, we think you might be the pefect fit for us. Made in Montréal, Kalooba is the exclusive clothing and accessories distributor of Loogart CityLine designs.
